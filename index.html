Regulations and Laws

A quick-search function could possibly be added in the future. Doubtful but possible. I will also only be including the ones that I most commonly find used on the station. Not EVERY law is listed here. (That and I feel like this section is possibly useless? We will see.)

- - - - - Low level infractions - - - - -

Trespassing

To be in an area which a person does not have access to. Suggested Sentence: 3 minutes, returning of stolen item to the owner or department.

Key Words - Trespass - Break in - Sneak in

Petty Theft

To take items from areas one does not have access to, or to take items belonging to others or the station as a whole. Suggested Sentence: 3 minutes.

Key Words - Stealing - Theif - Theft

Minor Assault

To use, or threaten, physical force against someone, without intent to kill or seriously injure. Suggested Sentence: 4 minutes.

Key Words - Punch - Hurt - Assault

- - - - - Medium level infractions - - - - -

Resist Arrest or Sparking a Manhunt

To not cooperate with an officer who attempts a proper arrest, or to cause a manhunt by hiding from security. Suggested Sentence: 10 minutes.

Key Words - Resisting - Manhunt - Refusing

Infiltration

To attempt to, or successfully, enter a high-security area without authorisation. Suggested Sentence: 15 minutes.

Key Words - Bridge/Armory/Vault - Break in - Theft

Assault

To cause severe injury to another employee. Suggested Sentence: 20 minutes.

Key Words - Hurt - Punch - Shoot - Assault

Contraband

To possess, use, or distribute contraband items, including drugs. Suggested Sentence: 15 minutes, confiscation of said items.

Key Words - Drugs - Weapon - Contraband

Grand Theft

To steal items that are dangerous, of a high value, or a sensitive nature.. Suggested Sentence: 15 minutes, confiscation of stolen items.

Key Words - Captain's Spare - Teleporter - Vault

- - - - - High Severity Infractions - - - - -

Murder

To kill someone, or attempt to kill someone, with premeditated malicious intent. Suggested Sentence: Holding until judgement.

Key words - Murder - Death - Kill

Kidnapping and Hostage Taking

To take away or transport a victim against the victim's will. Suggested sentence: 30 minutes.

Key Words - Hostage - Kidnapping - Evil

Terrorist Acts

To engage in maliciously destructive actions, which seriously threaten the crew or station. Suggested Sentence: Holding until judgement.

Key Words - Bombing - Explosive - Damage
